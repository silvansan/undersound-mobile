# UnderSound Mobile

## Listen to live event audio from your phone

UnderSound Mobile lets listeners join live event audio channels by scanning a QR code.

It is designed for interpretation, accessibility audio, conferences, venues, and live events where people need a simple way to listen from their own phone.

[Download Android APK](YOUR_APK_DOWNLOAD_LINK_HERE)

Android only for now. iOS support is planned.

---

## How It Works

### 1. Scan

Open the app and scan an UnderSound listener QR code at the event.

### 2. Choose

The app loads the public event and channel information automatically.

### 3. Listen

Start live audio with low-latency WebRTC playback and notification controls.

---

## Features

- Scan UnderSound listener QR codes
- Listen to live audio channels from events
- Low-latency WebRTC playback
- Save favorite listener channels
- Playback notification with pause and mute controls
- Manual link entry from the QR screen
- Fallback stream support when provided by the event server

## Installing on Android

Since UnderSound Mobile is currently distributed directly from this website, Android may ask you to allow installs from your browser or file manager.

This is normal for apps downloaded outside the Google Play Store.

1. Download the APK using the button above.
2. Open the downloaded file on your Android device.
3. If prompted, allow your browser or file manager to install unknown apps.
4. Install UnderSound Mobile.
5. Open the app and scan an UnderSound listener QR code.

---

## Privacy

UnderSound Mobile does not require a user account.

Camera access is used only for scanning QR codes. The app does not record, store, or upload camera images or video.

The app connects to the UnderSound server referenced by the listener link in order to load public event information and play live audio.

Saved favorites are stored locally on your device.
